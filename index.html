Kevin Vander Horn

Software Engineer & Game Creator

Born and raised in New Jersey, my passion for programming was acquired in an intro computer science class at Warren Hills Regional High School. I was able to obtain a great object-oriented programming foundation as well as the confidence I needed to pursue a STEM-related career. I then went on the further my studies at Arizona State University with a Bachelor’s in Software Engineering.

A large portion of my enjoyment in life stems from problem solving and learning new ways to create or improve my skills. Collaborating on projects, being a part of a team, and learning from fellow colleagues is one of the greatest aspects of being an engineer, and that is what I hope to continue to achieve in the gaming industry.
